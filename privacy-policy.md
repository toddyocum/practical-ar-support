 Privacy Policy

**PractiCal AR**
*Last updated: February 26, 2026*

## Overview

PractiCal AR ("the App") is an augmented reality tool designed for FIRST® Robotics Competition (FRC®) teams to calibrate practice fields using AprilTag detection. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

**PractiCal AR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The App operates entirely on your device.

Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking:** The App does not use any analytics frameworks or tracking tools.
- **No crash reporting:** The App does not send crash reports or diagnostic data to any server.
- **No network requests:** The App does not communicate with any external servers or services.
- **No account required:** The App does not require you to create an account or sign in.

## Camera and Sensor Usage

The App requires access to your device's camera and motion sensors to provide augmented reality functionality, including AprilTag detection and field calibration. Camera and sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Data Export

The App allows you to export field calibration data in JSON formats compatible with WPILib and Limelight. These exported files contain only field calibration measurements and do not include any personally identifiable information. Exported files are saved locally to your device, and you control how and where they are shared.

## Third-Party Services

PractiCal AR does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the App does not collect any data, changes are expected to be infrequent.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/toddyocum/practical-ar-support/issues).
