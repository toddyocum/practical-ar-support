# PractiCal AR Support

Welcome to the support page for **PractiCal AR**, an augmented reality tool for FIRST® Robotics Competition (FRC®) teams to calibrate practice fields using AprilTag detection.

## Getting Help

If you encounter a bug, have a question, or would like to request a feature, please [open an issue](https://github.com/toddyocum/practical-ar-support/issues) on our GitHub repository.

When reporting a bug, it's helpful to include:

- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
- Your iOS version
- A description of what you expected to happen and what actually happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What devices are supported?**
PractiCal AR requires an iPhone or iPad with ARKit support running iOS 16.0 or later.

**What are the supported export formats?**
The App can export field calibration data in WPILib JSON and Limelight JSON formats.

**Does the App require an internet connection?**
No. PractiCal AR works entirely offline.

**Does the App collect any personal data?**
No. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) for details.

## Contact

For support inquiries, please use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/toddyocum/practical-ar-support/issues).
